CMF Phone 1 By Nothing

Nothing Technology Limited is a British consumer electronics company founded in London in 2020 by Carl Pei. Its mission is to make technology feel exciting again through minimalist design and clean software.

The "CMF by Nothing" brand focuses on affordable, well-designed products like smartphones, earbuds, and smartwatches while maintaining Nothing's design philosophy.

The CMF Phone 1 is an affordable smartphone known for its customizable design, 120Hz AMOLED display, and clean Nothing OS experience. It was released in July 2024.

Key Features

  • Modular customizable back design
  • Excellent value for price
  • Clean Nothing OS 2.6 software
  • 120Hz AMOLED display & 5000mAh battery
